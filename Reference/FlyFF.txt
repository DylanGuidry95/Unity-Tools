Level 1 - 14 Vagrant Class
Level 15 - 59 Second Job
Level 130 - 150(Cap) Third Job

When a character reaches level 120 they can become a master and return to level 60 with enhance abilitys and when they reach level 121 they become a hero.

When level is reached to change job character no longer gains exp until a new job is choosen

Every level you are given two skill points to assign to any of the following stats
	Str - Increases attack damage
	Sta - Increases Defense
	Dex - Increases atk speed and crit chance
	Int - Increases magic damage
Also when you level up you are given two other skill points to be assigned to abilities that are a part of your current job or any job you have had
previously
Abilties
	- Can be assigned up to 10 points and each point acts like a level for that ability increasing the damage it does or if the ability is a buff
	it increases duration and stats the buff gives. 

Jobs
Every sub job can use skills from any of the characters previous jobs
